Fundación Legis

About us

The Fundación Legis was born from the commitment of Vox Legis Abogados and Legis Investment with human dignity and access to justice.

Mission

Promote opportunities and rights for vulnerable people.

Vision

A world where justice and protection reach everyone without exception.

Values

Ethics, justice, transparency, dignity and solidarity.

Our work

Areas

Child protection

Projects for education, nutrition and comprehensive protection of children in developing countries.

Criminal and penitentiary assistance

Legal defense, reintegration programs and human rights advocacy for people deprived of liberty.

Direct impact

Beneficiaries

Our work reaches children, families and people without access to justice who face situations of vulnerability.
