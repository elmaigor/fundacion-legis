About us

Fundación Legis was born from the commitment of Vox Legis Abogados and Legis Investment to defend human dignity and access to justice.

Mission

To promote life opportunities for vulnerable children and guarantee criminal and penitentiary assistance to those who cannot afford it.

Vision

A world where justice and child protection are a reality for everyone, regardless of origin or resources.

Values

Ethics, justice, transparency, dignity and social commitment.

Areas of work

Two complementary lines of impact.

Child protection in developing countries

We support health, education and protection projects for children in highly vulnerable contexts. We work with local partners for sustainability and cultural respect.

  • Primary care and nutrition.
  • Schooling and educational support.
  • Protection against abuse and exploitation.

Criminal and penitentiary assistance

Legal guidance, defence and penitentiary support for people without resources, with a strong human-rights focus.

  • Advice and defence in criminal proceedings.
  • Penitentiary assistance and benefits follow-up.
  • Referral to complementary social resources.
