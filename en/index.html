Fundación Legis

About us

Fundación Legis was born from the commitment of Vox Legis Abogados and Legis Investment to human dignity and access to justice.

Mission

Promote life opportunities for vulnerable children and ensure criminal and penitentiary assistance for those without resources.

Vision

A world where justice and child protection are a reality for everyone.

Values

Ethics, justice, transparency, dignity and social commitment.

Areas

Areas of action

Child protection

  • Primary care and nutrition.
  • Schooling and educational support.
  • Protection from abuse and exploitation.

Criminal and penitentiary assistance

  • Defense in criminal proceedings.
  • Penitentiary assistance and benefits.
  • Referral to social resources.
