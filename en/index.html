Fundación Legis

About us

Fundación Legis was born from the commitment of Vox Legis Abogados and Legis Investment to defend human dignity and access to justice.

Mission

To promote life opportunities for vulnerable children and guarantee criminal and penitentiary assistance to those who cannot afford it.

Vision

A world where justice and child protection are a reality for everyone.

Values

Ethics, justice, transparency, dignity and social commitment.

Programmes

Areas of work

Child protection

  • Primary care and nutrition.
  • Schooling and educational support.
  • Protection against abuse and exploitation.

Criminal & penitentiary assistance

  • Defence in criminal proceedings.
  • Penitentiary assistance and benefits follow-up.
  • Referral to complementary social resources.

Beneficiaries

If you need assistance, this is how the process works:

  1. Fill in the request form.
  2. Our team will assess it according to the Legal Aid Act.
  3. We will contact you to schedule the first appointment.
